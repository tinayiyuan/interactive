A Boat                    O beautiful
                 was the werewolf
                  in his evil forest.
to the carnival
               We took him
and he started
                               crying
                when                he saw
the                Ferris wheel.
Elect               ric
                                             green and red tears
                              flowed down

He                looked
like a                boat
out on the                dark
